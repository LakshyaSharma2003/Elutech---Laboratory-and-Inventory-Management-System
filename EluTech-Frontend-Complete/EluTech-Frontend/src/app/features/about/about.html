Who We Are

Precision You Can
Trust. Every Time.

EluTech Laboratory & Calibration Services is a leading NABL-accredited testing and calibration laboratory based in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Founded with the mission of delivering accurate, reliable, and timely analytical services, EluTech has grown into one of the most trusted names in the laboratory science sector across Rajasthan and neighbouring states.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses advanced instrumentation for chemical, microbiological, environmental, and material testing, operated by a team of qualified scientists, engineers, and technicians. We adhere strictly to ISO/IEC 17025 standards — the international benchmark for laboratory competence — ensuring every result we deliver is technically sound and legally defensible.

From pharmaceutical manufacturers ensuring product purity, to construction companies verifying material strength, to municipal bodies monitoring water quality — EluTech serves as the trusted scientific partner across a diverse range of sectors.

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Jitendra Kumar Sharma is the visionary founder and CEO of EluTech Laboratory & Calibration Services. With deep expertise in laboratory sciences, metrology, and quality management systems, he established EluTech with a clear purpose — to bring world-class testing and calibration capabilities to Rajasthan's industrial and institutional ecosystem.

Under his leadership, EluTech achieved NABL accreditation and built a reputation for scientific integrity, timely delivery, and customer-centric service. Mr. Sharma believes that accurate measurement is the foundation of quality in every industry — and has shaped EluTech's culture around this principle.

He actively engages with industry bodies, regulatory authorities, and academic institutions to promote the importance of quality testing in manufacturing, environment protection, and public health.
